NASA EXOPLANET ARCHIVE
EXOPLANET

NASA's Exoplanet Exploration Program has several key goals:

1. Discover and characterize planetary systems around nearby stars.

2. Find Earth-like planets that could potentially support life.

3. Search for evidence of life beyond our solar system by analyzing exoplanet atmospheres.

4. Advance our understanding of planetary formation and evolution.

5. Explore the possibility of habitable worlds beyond our own.
